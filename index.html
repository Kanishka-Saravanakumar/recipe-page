Spicy Mango Chicken Tacos

A fusion of bold Mexican flavors with a tropical Indian twist — crispy spiced chicken, fresh mango salsa, and a creamy sriracha drizzle. Eye-catching and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 14.3 ounces chicken breast (thinly sliced)
  • 8 pieces small flour tortillas
  • 1 piece ripe mango (diced)
  • 0.5 piece red onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 tablespoons fresh coriander (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded purple cabbage

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: Mix chicken with spices and olive oil. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Make the mango salsa: Combine mango, onion, coriander, and lime juice.
  3. Make the sriracha drizzle: Mix sriracha and mayonnaise.
  4. Cook the chicken: Cook for 5–6 minutes per side until done.
  5. Warm the tortillas: Heat for 20–40 seconds per side.
  6. Assemble the tacos: Add cabbage, chicken, salsa, drizzle, and coriander.
